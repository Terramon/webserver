About the Mod


The Terramon Mod is a cross between Terraria and the awesome Pokémon franchise. The mod adds many catchable Pokémon to your classic Terraria adventure and your mission is to befriend them all! We're aiming to make this mod a smashing success with Pokémon fans all over the Terraria community with handcrafted, quality-over-quantity content. Try out the mod today!
